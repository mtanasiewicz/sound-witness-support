# SoundWitness

  **AI-powered noise detection & evidence reports for macOS.**

  SoundWitness scans surveillance video recordings, detects nuisance sounds using AI, and generates professional PDF reports
  with nuisance scoring — ready for administrative and legal use.

  ## Features

  - **AI Sound Detection** — PANN CNN14 neural network analyzes audio and classifies 527 types of sounds
  - **6 Sound Profiles** — Pets, Nature, People, Traffic, Noise, or All Sounds
  - **PDF Reports** — Hourly charts, nuisance scoring, continuous event analysis, SHA-256 integrity verification
  - **Episode Management** — Review, soft-delete, or hard-delete detected sound episodes
  - **Video Assembly** — Automatically concatenates detected episodes into an output video
  - **Append Workflow** — Add new video files to existing projects with collision detection
  - **25 Languages** — Full UI localization (EN, BG, CS, DA, DE, EL, ES, ET, FI, FR, GA, HR, HU, IT, LT, LV, MT, NL, NO, PL,
  PT, RO, SK, SL, SV)
  - **Privacy First** — All processing happens locally on your Mac. No data leaves your device.

  ## Requirements

  - macOS 14.0 (Sonoma) or later
  - Apple Silicon or Intel Mac

  ## Privacy Policy

  SoundWitness processes all video and audio files **entirely on your device**. The app:

  - Does **not** collect, transmit, or store any personal data
  - Does **not** use analytics, tracking, or telemetry
  - Does **not** connect to external servers (except Apple's App Store for in-app purchases, handled entirely by Apple)
  - Requires file access only for the video files you explicitly select

  Your files never leave your Mac.
