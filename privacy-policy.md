# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** March 29, 2026
**Last updated:** March 29, 2026

SoundWitness ("the App") is developed by Marcin Tanasiewicz ("Developer", "we", "us"). This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your information when you use the App.

## Data We Collect

**We do not collect any data.** The App processes all video and audio files entirely on your device. No personal data, usage data, analytics, or telemetry is collected, transmitted, or stored by us.

Specifically, the App:

- Does **not** collect, transmit, or store any personal data
- Does **not** use analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind
- Does **not** connect to external servers
- Does **not** create user accounts or require registration
- Processes all files locally on your Mac — your files never leave your device

## Apple App Store

The App uses Apple's StoreKit framework for in-app purchases (subscriptions and one-time purchases). These transactions are handled entirely by Apple and are subject to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). We do not have access to your payment information.

## File Access

The App accesses only the video files you explicitly select through the macOS file picker. This access is governed by macOS App Sandbox and requires your explicit permission. We do not access any other files on your device.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms.

## Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will post the revised version here with an updated "Last updated" date. Since we do not collect any contact information, we cannot notify you directly — please review this page periodically.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please [open an issue](https://github.com/mtanasiewicz/sound-witness-support/issues) or contact us at the email address listed on our App Store developer page.
